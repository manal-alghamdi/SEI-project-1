Welcome

You probably already know how to play Tic-Tac-Toe. It's a simple game, right? Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
